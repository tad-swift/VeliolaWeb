1. Personal Information Collection and Use

If you grant BeautySpot permission, we may collect and use personal information from you as follows.

  • Location Services
    Purpose: BeautySpot needs your location to show you businesses in your area

  • Camera
    Purpose: For uploading a cover photo

  • Photos
    Purpose: BeautySpot wants to upload a photo from your library

  • Calendars
    Purpose: BeautySpot wants to add appointments to your calendar BeautySpot wants to add appointments to your calendar.

Please note that you can decline a permission or revoke it after you granted it in the iOS settings. If you do so, we will not collect your personal information related to that permission. However, the functionality of BeautySpot may be limited as a result.

We may receive technical information about your device, for example, its IP address or operating system.

3. Tracking Technologies

4. Social Logins

BeautySpot does not use any social login functionality.


5. In-app Purchase Information

BeautySpot offers in-app purchases. However, any in-app purchase is processed by Apple via the App Store and your App Store account. In particular, we will not store your credit card or other payment information on our servers.


6. Children's Personal Information

Users of BeautySpot must be at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child, we will take all reasonable steps to delete it as soon as possible. If you become aware that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at the contact information below.

7. How Long We Keep Your Personal Information

We keep all the information for as long as the BeautySpot platform exists, unless users request for their data to be deleted.


8. How We Protect Your Personal Information

The personal data we collect is securely uploaded to our servers and stay on our servers. Your personal data is never transferred outside of our servers unless it is to view it by you, within the BeautySpot app. We use encryption to protect personal information stored and transmitted online. Only members of the BeautySpot team who need the personal information to perform a specific function have access to such. The computers and servers on which we store personal information are kept in a secure environment.


9. Policy Changes

When we make material changes to this privacy policy, for example, due to app updates or changes in the law, we will post the updated policy here. The policy will always apply in the version at the time of your use of BeautySpot even if you downloaded BeautySpot at a time when a previous policy version was applicable.


10. Accessibility

This website conforms to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1. However, if you are having difficulties obtaining information from it, please contact us at the contact information below. We will try to make the information available to you in another format and answer any question that you may have.
